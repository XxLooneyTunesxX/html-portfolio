Top 3 Movies of all Time

A Cinderella Story

It's lighthearted, funny, and personally nostalgic. A cute romance.

White Chicks

I die of laughter every single time. Seriously funny stuff, and also nostalgic. Is there a pattern here?

The Greatest Showman

I love circuses, so this obviously makes the cut. I got weirdly emotional watching this for the first time, and then proceeded to watch it around 3 more times that year.
